# 🦅 Regula Pro Max v1.2.0 – AI‑Powered Release

## 📦 Download

Click `Regula_ProMax_Setup.exe` below to download the installer.  
Double‑click to run – no additional dependencies required.

---

## 🔓 Free Features (Default)

- Context‑Aware Rule Engine – understands context, not just keywords
- Batch Folder Scanning – scan all `.docx` files in a folder with one click
- Team Heatmap – visual overview of violation distribution across files
- Custom Rule Management – add, edit, or delete your own rules
- Basic PDF Reports – export formal compliance reports with violation details
- 100% Offline – your data never leaves your machine

---

## ⭐ Pro Features (Activated with Pro License)

- OCR Image Recognition – extract text from screenshots and images
- 50+ Rule Templates – pre‑built rules for E‑Commerce, Finance, Medical, and General
- Advanced PDF Reports – includes pie charts and visual analytics
- Multilingual Scanning – supports English, Japanese, and Korean (in addition to Chinese)
- Command‑Line Mode – CI/CD integration for automated compliance checks
- Debug Logging – `debug.txt` captures runtime errors for troubleshooting

---

## 👑 Pro Max Features (Activated with Pro Max License)

- **🤖 AI Semantic Understanding** – powered by sentence‑transformers, understands the true intent of your copy
- **📊 Smart Violation Classification** – automatically categorises violations into groups like "False Advertising", "Medical Claims", "Discriminatory Language", and more
- **💡 Intelligent Fix Suggestions** – provides context‑aware suggestions based on semantic matching
- **👑 Pro Max Badge** – displays a "PRO MAX" identity badge within the application

## 🔑 How to Activate

### Activate Pro
Send your **Machine ID** to: **Regula_Official@outlook.com**

### Activate Pro Max (Pro must be activated first)
After activating Pro, click **"👑 升级 Pro Max"**, copy your Machine ID, and send it to us.  
You will receive a Pro Max activation code.
