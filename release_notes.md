# 🦅 Regula Pro v1.0.0 – First Pro Release

## 📦 Download

Click the `Regula_Setup.exe` asset below to download the installer.  
Double‑click to run – no additional dependencies required.

---

## 🔓 Free Features (Default)

- **Context‑Aware Rule Engine** – flags violations intelligently, not just keyword matching  
- **Batch Folder Scanning** – scan all `.docx` files in a folder with one click  
- **Team Heatmap** – visual overview of violation distribution across files  
- **Custom Rule Management** – add, edit, or delete your own rules  
- **Basic PDF Reports** – export formal compliance reports with violation details  
- **100% Offline** – your data never leaves your machine

---

## ⭐ Pro Features (Activated with License Key)

- **OCR Image Recognition** – extract text from screenshots and images for scanning  
- **50+ Rule Templates** – pre‑built rules for E‑Commerce, Finance, Medical, and General industries  
- **Advanced PDF Reports** – includes pie charts and visual analytics  
- **Multilingual Scanning** – supports English, Japanese, and Korean (in addition to Chinese)  
- **Command‑Line Mode** – CI/CD integration for automated compliance checks  
- **Debug Logging** – `debug.txt` captures runtime errors for troubleshooting

---

## 🔑 How to Activate Pro

1. Launch Regula after installation.  
2. Click **"🚀 Upgrade to Pro"** in the top‑right corner.  
3. Copy your **Machine ID** and email it to:  
   **Regula_Official@outlook.com**  
4. You will receive an activation code.  
5. Paste the code into the activation dialog and click **Activate**.

> **Note**: The license key is tied to your specific machine and cannot be shared.
